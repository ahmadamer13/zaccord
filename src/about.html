What is 3DJordanPrint?

A service and webshop producing 3D‑printed products — on‑demand printing and prototyping for both businesses and individuals.

Services

You can purchase ready‑made items from the homepage, or request custom printing for your own model. We also create lithophanes from a single photo, and we offer prototyping and custom prints tailored to your needs.

Our mission

We serve customers who need small runs, prototypes, or models quickly and affordably but lack the in‑house tools to make them.

Who we serve

Individuals and companies that need fast, cost‑effective prototypes or models that are difficult or expensive to make using other methods.

Minimalism & simplicity

We believe simplicity is the ultimate sophistication. We focus on a smooth purchasing experience with a clean, easy‑to‑use interface.

We care about the environment

Our catalog items are printed with biodegradable filament to reduce environmental impact. We also use eco‑friendly void fill for packaging.

Open‑source mindset

To promote 3D printing, we share parts of our code openly on GitHub to inspire others to explore the technology.
