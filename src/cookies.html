Cookie Policy

Overview

We use cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. Some cookies are necessary for the site to function; others help us analyze usage and improve performance. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, or collect anonymous analytics. Cookies are not programs and cannot run code.

2. How we use cookies

- Essential cookies: required for core features (cart, checkout, security).
- Analytics cookies: help us understand how the site is used (aggregated, anonymous).
- Preference cookies: remember choices like language and previously viewed items.

3. Third‑party cookies

We may use third‑party services (e.g., analytics) that set their own cookies. These providers have their own privacy policies.

4. Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling essential cookies may break site functionality. See help pages for your browser on how to manage cookies.
